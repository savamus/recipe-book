# Borscht

## Ingredients
- 500 g beef
- 2 potatoes
- 1 beetroot
- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- Salt, pepper

## Steps
1. Boil the beef until tender.
2. Add chopped vegetables.
3. Cook until vegetables are soft.
4. Add tomato paste, salt, and pepper to taste.
5. Serve with sour cream.
